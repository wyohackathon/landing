The new 4-story Engineering Education and Research Building is the latest addition to the University of Wyoming College of Engineering and Applied Science. Comprised of research and teaching lab spaces, faculty offices, a medium classroom, a large auditorium, and collaboration spaces, the building is shared by multiple disciplines of engineering department including civil, architectural, mechanical, electrical, computer science, atmospheric, chemical and petroleum engineering programs. The grand 4-story atrium also features a “floating” glass and steel staircase.
