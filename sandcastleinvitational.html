Sandcastle Invitational

 

What: Think your startup has what it takes to compete with the best? Then the Sandcastle competition wants you and your team. A startup qualifier competition, Sandcastle is seeking the best and most promising blockchain, AI, and other technology startups to compete in Laramie and then Dubai for the Sandcastle Challenge finale. Cash prizes are offered for the best presentations and companies. An exciting and thrilling experience for both spectators and competitors.

 

Theme: Launching Impact—Digital Technologies

 

Where: View the event live in Laramie, Wyoming or from anywhere in the world via remote live stream.

 

When: September 22, 2021, 5 p.m.- 8 p.m., Marian H. Rochelle Gateway Center, 22nd and Grand, Laramie.
