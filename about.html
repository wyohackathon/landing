The 2021 Wyoming Blockchain Stampede comprises multiple free virtual events, covering major blockchain themes such as, sources, impact, and implementation that look to unite worldwide participants with thought leaders, legislators, and changemakers at the forefront of major impacts to the world’s future in blockchain technology.


“We’ll explore the impact of digital innovations to reduce costs and improve profits, we’ll examine sources that prove the identity and provenance of individuals and assets, and we’re inviting participants to join discussions on implementing new laws and technologies,” says Rich Kopcho, producer of the event.


The Wyoming Blockchain Stampede events will include:


  • Select Committee (9/21- 9/22) - The legislators' select committee on blockchain, financial technology, and digital innovation will hear testimony from the public on opportunities to improve the laws.

  • Law Conference (9/23) - Learn how the new laws in Wyoming impact blockchain and crypto businesses and help form the ecosystem. CLE credits are also available to those who want them. Come learn from the best legal minds in the blockchain industry!

  • Sandcastle Invitational (9/22) - Sandcastle seeks the best and most promising blockchain startups in the world to compete for the top cash prize.

  • DAO Day (9/24) - Technical workshops and speakers explaining what DAO's are and the role they will play in our future. Plus, how Wyoming is leading the way in terms of DAO regulation.

  • WyoHackathon (9/24 - 9/26) - The WyoHackathon presents challenges and bounties for developers, entrepreneurs, and business professionals. Use the weekend to come up with the best solution to the problems presented.

  • Business Conference (9/23) - A full day of conversations helping attendees understand business opportunities of blockchain and use cases.

  • Finance Conference (9/25) - Take a deep dive into the evolving world of finance. Learn how blockchain technology is pushing decentralized finance and what impact that will have. Besides this, learn about other rapid innovations that are taking place in this area. CE credits will be available to those who want them. This should be a great learning opportunity to see how fast finance is changing and how you can prepare yourself for that future.

“Whether you are just interested in learning more or are a pro with blockchain technologies, there will be something for every level at these events,” says Kopcho. “We want everyone to join the blockchain conversation and learn more about the opportunities it has to offer.”
