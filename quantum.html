Quantum Entanglement

Emergent IMPACT


What: Quantum entanglement is a physical phenomenon occurring when a group of particles interact. An apt metaphor for bringing together the DOE, UWYO, IBM Research, VC’s and entrepreneurs.


Emergence occurs when the group is observed to have properties its parts do not have on their own. We anticipate important economic IMPACT could occur in Wyoming’s future.


Where: Livestreaming into our Virtual Reality 2D/3D environment accessible worldwide via VR Headset or HTML5 browser.
